How to play

Goal

You, the Russian cosmonaut, must leave the moonbase by running around collecting enough moonrocket fuel (50 or more of the red/green drops). Watch out, the Russian moonzombies are also trying to leave the moonbase, but they don't understand the point of rocket ships, so they'll end up punching it until it explodes.

Controls

  • Arrow keys - move the cosmonaut
  • (held) A, S, D, F - displays build cost of each tower in upper left
  • (held) Spacebar - enters build mode
  • (held) Spacebar + (pressed) A, S, D, F - builds the corrosponding tower in the area marked in build mode

Towers

There are four buildable towers:

Pile O'Trash
This space trash has high defense and serves as a great distraction for the zombies from your zappy cannons
Zappy Cannon
This nuclear powered laser cannon shoots lasers and zombies because that's it's job. Very low defense.
Neutron Bomb
Also known as the "capitalist bomb", it has a short fuse and kills a lot of zombies around it.
Nuke
Kaboom and all the zombies die.

Notes

  • There are three drops from zombies: wood (tan-brown colored), metal (purple-blue colored), and fuel (red-green colored)
  • There are two types of zombies; be on the lookout for the elusive bongo zombie!
  • Zombies teleport in from somewhere because we thought the teleporter animation was really cool.
  • FMP stands for "Fluid Moon Pounds", which is the unit of measurement we believe futuristic Russian cosmonauts will use whilst fighting Russian cosmozombies.
